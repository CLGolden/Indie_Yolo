Who this Program is For

Local Businesses

If you have a product or service that is only available for customers in or around Yolo County, CA, your business is considered a 'Local Business.'

Minority Business Owners

This program is for "Minority" business owners which include people of color, indiginous or Asian people, and women, especially single moms.

Willingness to Learn

This is a beginners program. Business owners will be given the education and mentorship needed to run their marketing program with confidence.
