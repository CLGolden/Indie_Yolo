Program Overview

This is a very specific program designed for small, local business owners just beginning to do online marketing. Normally, a program like this would start at $3,000. As a non-profit organization, our goal is to provide this program for free to minority business owners.

This 6-Month Marketing and Mentoring Program consists of...

  • The development of a simple, static website
  • Getting established on Google Search and Maps
  • Education and mentoring on marketing basics and internet safety
