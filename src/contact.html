Grant Application

The grant application is a 4-step process. The application below is the first step, meant to show you have an interest.

In Step 2, you'll be sent a request for more detailed information including some photos of your product or service.

Step 3 is a Zoom call where we can address any final questions or concerns and discuss the process of working together.

Step 4 is the contract.
