PLEASE NOTE: We are not ready to accept tax-deductable donations at this time. Currently, we are looking for our first program participant. If you know someone who might be a good candidate for this program, please send them our way!

There are two ways to donate

  • Sponsor a Local, Minority-Owned Business
  • Make a General Donation

Sponsor a Local, Minority-Owned Business

Are you in a position to sponsor a local, minority business owner's marketing success? "Minority" includes people of color, Indigenous or Asian people, and women, especially single moms.

Have the business owner fill in the Application checking the box that asks if they have a sponsor.

In the second step of the process, we'll ask the candidate for your contact info and coordinate with you on progress updates and payment schedule.

This 6-month program retails for $3,000. As a non-profit with a volunteer web developer, we can offer the program for $1,800. Payments can be made monthly ($ 300) and can be discontinued if the recipient quits the program early.

General Donation

You can make a general donation to this program by credit card. Program income and expense statements are available on our Corporate Info page.
