There are two ways to donate

Sponsor a Local, Minority-Owned Business

Are you in a position to sponsor a local, minority business owner's marketing success? "Minority" includes people of color, indiginous or Asian people, and women, especially single moms.

Have the business owner fill in the Grant Application checking the box that asks if they have a sponsor.

In the second step of the process, we'll ask the candidate for your contact info and coordinate with you on progress updates and payment schedule.

This 6-month program retails for $3,000. Since, as a non-profit, we don't have to pay federal taxes, we offer it for $2,520. Payments can be made monthly ($ 420) and can be discontinued if the student quits the program early.

General Donation

You can make a general donation to this program by credit card. Program income and expense statements are available on our Documents page.
