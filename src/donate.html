There are two ways to donate

  • Make a General Donation
  • Sponsor a Local Business Owner

General Donation

You can make a general donation to this program by credit card. Program income and expense statements are available on our Corporate Info page.

Sponsor a Local, Small Business

Are you in a position to sponsor a local, woman- or minority-owned business owner's marketing success? "Minority" includes people of color, Indigenous or Asian people, and women, especially single moms.

Have the business owner fill in the Application checking the box that asks if they have a sponsor.

In the second step of the process, we'll ask the candidate for your contact info and coordinate with you on progress updates and payment schedule.

This 6-month program retails for $3,000. As a non-profit, we can offer the program for $1,800. Payments can be made monthly ($ 300) and can be discontinued if the recipient quits the program early.
